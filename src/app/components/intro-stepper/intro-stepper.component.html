Welcome to MoneyBuddy

What is MoneyBuddy?

MoneyBuddy is your personal finance assistant. It helps you manage debt, track expenses, and make smarter money decisions — all through a simple, chat-based experience.

Your Privacy

Your privacy is our top priority. All personal information you enter is 100% private, confidential, and never for sale.

We don't share your data with third parties, ever.

What You’ll Be Asked

In a quick chat, we’ll ask a few simple things:

  • Your monthly income
  • A description or summary of your debt
  • Your monthly expenses
  • Your savings and monthly contributions

No spreadsheets. No stress. Just a conversation.

 How MoneyBuddy Helps

Once you’ve shared your details, you can ask MoneyBuddy anything:

  • "How can I get out of debt fastest?"
  • "How should I organize recurring expenses across my cards?"
  • "Can I afford a $500/mo car?"
  • "Can I buy a $1,400 TV right now?"

And MoneyBuddy will respond like:
“Right now, based on your income, expenses, and goals, buying a $1,400 TV isn’t ideal. But if you reduce dining out by $150/month, you could afford a $1,300 TV in 3 months.”
